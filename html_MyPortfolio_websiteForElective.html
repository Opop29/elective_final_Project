ABOUT ME

Joshua Quidit

Joshua Quidit, a resident of Sankanan, Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon, is a creative individual with a passion for artistic expression. Born with an innate talent for drawing, Joshua has found solace and joy in the simplicity of a pencil and the boundless possibilities it offers. Early Life: Joshua's journey in the world of art began in the scenic landscapes of Bukidnon. Surrounded by the natural beauty of his hometown, he developed a deep appreciation for the arts and a keen eye for capturing the essence of his surroundings. Love for Drawing: One of Joshua's defining characteristics is his love for drawing, a craft he has honed with a humble pencil. Embracing traditional tools, Joshua believes that the tactile connection between hand and paper allows for a unique and intimate form of self-expression. His sketches, crafted with precision and care, reveal the intricate details of his imaginative mind. Artistic Philosophy: In a world increasingly dominated by technology, Joshua holds a distinctive perspective. While acknowledging the importance of technology in society, he remains rooted in the belief that the simplicity of a pencil provides a timeless and authentic connection to artistic expression. Joshua sees technology as a valuable tool, but he cherishes the tactile and organic nature of traditional art. Community Involvement: Beyond his personal artistic endeavors, Joshua actively engages with his community. He shares his passion by conducting local art workshops, encouraging others to explore their creativity and appreciate the beauty that surrounds them. Future Aspirations: Looking ahead, Joshua envisions a future where he continues to merge traditional and contemporary artistic approaches. He aspires to create a meaningful impact through his art, fostering a deeper connection between individuals and the world around them. In the quiet town of Sankanan, where the rolling hills meet the sky, Joshua Quidit's artistic journey unfolds, leaving an indelible mark on the canvas of his community and beyond.
